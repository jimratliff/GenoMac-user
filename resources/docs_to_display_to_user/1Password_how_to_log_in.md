# How to log in to 1Password.app
Launch 1Password and/or make it active.

## Log into 1Password
Log into your 1Password account. The best way to do this is:
- When you launch the desktop 1Password app for the first time, it will prominently display a "Sign in" button.
- ❑ Click the “Sign in” button.
  - This will display a sign-in dialog box, including a QR code you can use to make the sign-in process
    quicker.
- ❑ Get your iPhone.
  - ❑ Launch 1Password on iPhone
  - ❑ From the 1Password logo in the upper-left corner of the 1Password app on iPhone, select “Scan QR Code…”
  - ❑ Follow the instructions

## Return to terminal and acknowledge
- ❑ Type `done` to acknowledge that you’ve completed signing into 1Password.
